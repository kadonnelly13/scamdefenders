Andy Greenberg shares the story of a single individual and their journey to and
from a scam compound they were forced to work in. This is a must read to help
understand part of the dynamics and inner workers of how people get lured to
these compounds. It reveals how these criminal enterprises exploit both scam
victims who lose their life savings and enslaved workers forced to perpetrate
the frauds under brutal conditions.
